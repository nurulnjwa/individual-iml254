Inspiring Education for All

This is my high school. I have experienced and learned a lot while studying here. I will never forget the friends and teachers who taught me at this school. Every second of time that passes I will remember all their services.

After finishing school, I applied to UiTM Lendu Melaka. There I took the commerce stream as a pre-diploma certificate for 6 months and 1 semester.

After completing my pre-diploma, I continued my studies at UiTM Merbok Kedah. This was the first time I set foot in the north to continue my studies. Before I came here, my family was very surprised because I got an offer to go to Kedah, but for the sake of my studies, they accepted it even though I knew they were reluctant to allow me to continue my studies in Kedah. Here, I took the library informatics course and have now entered semester 4.
